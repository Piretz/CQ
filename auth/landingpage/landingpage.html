CIPHERION

Welcome to CIPHERION, a revolutionary learning platform designed exclusively for Information Technology and Computer Science students at Quezon City University. This gamified project transforms traditional programming lessons into an immersive, quest-driven experience.

Students will embark on exciting missions, tackling progressively challenging coding puzzles, collaborating on dynamic group projects, and mastering essential programming concepts through hands-on exploration. CIPHERION promotes teamwork, problem-solving, and creative thinking while fostering a vibrant community of future tech leaders.

Course Offer

Cipherion offers a diverse range of courses designed to equip IT students with essential skills for the tech industry. From foundational topics like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for building responsive and interactive websites to advanced programming in Java and Python, students gain hands-on experience in solving real-world problems. Additional courses in database management and game development provide opportunities to explore specialized fields, ensuring a well-rounded and engaging learning journey. Learn More
